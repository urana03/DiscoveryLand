Founded in 1994 by Mike Meldman,Discovey Land Company is
a US-based real estate developer and operator of private residential
club communities and resorts with a world-renowned portfolio of
domestic and international properties.

Our Mission

We don't build buildings but rather we build settings where families can create intergenerational memories and enrich their lives in exquisite and untouched sancturies.Everything we do is driven by the fundamental desire to create spaces where families can be together.

These settings offer a relaxed and comfortable sense of community featuring outstanding clubhouses,incredile residential offering, premier golf courses,welcoming staff,world-class culinary offerings and our custom Outdoor Pursuits program;all offering the highest of service standars to enrich your experience.

The Discovery Story

For almost three decades,Discovery has built communities that encourages both our development teams and members themselves to dream up the most fun experiences imaginable.The first development in Scottsdale set the standad for a Discovery community,complete with a championship golf course, stocked-to-the brim comfort stations,luxurious residential offerings,five-star service,and more; since then,each community has built on the fun from the one that came before.
